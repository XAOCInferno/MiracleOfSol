Starting Squads:
Champion, Demolition, Sorcerer, Heavy Weapons

Purpose:
 - Allow player to have fun with all they've learned by killing lots of weaker enemies and some tanks
 - Levels heros for final mission


Gameplay:
 - If sorc has the level 3 energy tree perk he will be able to teleport the party over gaps
 - Otherwise Champion has to jump to locations to open the gate
